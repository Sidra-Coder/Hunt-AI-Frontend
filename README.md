Hunt AI is a smart web application designed to help e-commerce store owners find trending "winning" products by analyzing market trends and customer sentiment. Built with a modern, Antigravity-inspired Z-layout, the platform streamlines the product discovery process.Core FunctionalityAI Hype Score: A predictive success gauge based on reviews and web traffic.Competitor Tracker: A real-time tool to compare pricing and stock status across major stores.Automated Discovery: Replaces manual searching with data-backed market analysis.Target AudienceRetailers: Independent Shopify store owners and drop shippers.Agencies: E-commerce marketing agencies looking for data-driven product insights.The Value PropositionBy automating product research, Hunt AI allows users to make informed inventory decisions without wasting time or capital on unpopular items
